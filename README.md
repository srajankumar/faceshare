## About

An Open Source Linktree Substitute. Build your awesome profile and easily share it with your connections for free! Create a standout online presence that reflects your uniqueness.

## Features

- **Create a cool Profile in minutes**  
  Connect your Instagram, Twitter, Linkedin, GitHub, Spotify, website, podcast, and more üåê

- **Share it with your friends**  
  Effortlessly share your personalized online hub with friends through a QR code, simplifying connectivity and ensuring seamless access to all your important links ü§ù

- **Explore and connecet with ease**  
  Search and connect with like-minded individuals, building your online presence within a network of shared interests and passions üåü
